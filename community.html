Changing Climate Change

The planet's climate is always changing. Human activities such as fossil fuel burning, cutting down forests, and generating power are the main cause of those changes. Due to those human activities, we have many bad climate change influences. Although through collaboration we can reverse our bad influences. Changing Climate Change is a force within our community that does a variaty of things, to learn more, visit our discord or reach out!

What is Environmental Ease?

Taking care of the environment can be difficult. It doesn't have to be though. Environmental Ease is what we like to call it. It's easy! Environmental Ease is a program in progress that allows users to share tips, connect to their local environmental organizations, and help spread awareness of our environment. If you're interested in helping organizing, reach out or visit our discord!

Kappa in the Communtiy

Our mascot isn't something you'd typically stumble across. It's a friendly green kappa that is here to help you and the world get healthier! Kappa's are japanese mythological creatures that live in the ponds and rivers of Japan. They are known for their vivid green color, sometimes believed to have powers, and seen in some Japanese animations!
