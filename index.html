Going Kappa Green

The planet deserves to be in good health and to be green. "Green as a Kappa!" Our organization, called Going Kappa Green, or GKG, swears allegiance to sustaining the earth and its ecosystems as much as possible.

Climate Change

The planet's climate is always changing. Human activities such as fossil fuel burning, cutting down forests, and generating power are the main cause of those changes. Due to those human activities, we have many bad climate change influences. Although through collaboration we can reverse our bad influences. Interested in how? Check our COMMUNITY Tab

Environmental Ease

Taking care of the environment can be difficult. It doesn't have to be though. Environmental Ease is what we like to call it. It's easy! Environmental Ease is a program in progress that allows users to share tips, connect to their local environmental organizations, and help spread awareness of our environment. Check our COMMUNITY Tab for more info.
